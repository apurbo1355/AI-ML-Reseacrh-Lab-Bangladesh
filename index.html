// About the Lab

Pioneering AI Research
in South Asia

AI & ML Research Lab Bangladesh is an independent, interdisciplinary research institute dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence and machine learning research with a focus on applications relevant to the South Asian context.

Founded by a team of passionate researchers, professors, and industry experts, our lab bridges the gap between cutting-edge academic research and real-world deployment in healthcare, agriculture, education, and governance.

  • Open, reproducible, and collaborative research culture
  • Strong ties with industry partners and government bodies
  • Dedicated mentorship for emerging Bangladeshi AI researchers
  • Focus on low-resource and multilingual AI for Bangla language
  • International conference participation and publication support

🎯 Mission

To conduct world-class AI & ML research that addresses the unique challenges of Bangladesh, while training the next generation of AI leaders.

👁 Vision

To become the leading AI research hub in South Asia, contributing to global knowledge while driving local innovation and technological sovereignty.

⚡ Focus Areas

Bangla NLP, Crop Disease Detection, Healthcare AI, Climate Modeling, Educational Technology, and Responsible AI Governance.

// Research Areas

What We Investigate

Our research spans multiple domains of AI and ML, from foundational theory to applied systems.

🗣️
Natural Language Processing
Bangla language models, sentiment analysis, machine translation, named entity recognition, and low-resource language AI for South Asian languages.
NLP · LLM · Bangla AI
👁️
Computer Vision
Object detection, image segmentation, medical image analysis, satellite imagery processing, and real-time video understanding systems.
CV · Detection · Segmentation
🏥
Healthcare AI
Disease prediction, clinical decision support, diagnostic imaging AI, drug discovery, and patient data privacy with federated learning.
HealthAI · Diagnostics · FL
🌾
Agricultural Intelligence
Crop disease detection, yield prediction, smart irrigation, pest recognition, and precision agriculture using drone imagery and IoT sensors.
AgriAI · Smart Farming
🔍
Explainable AI (XAI)
Building transparent and interpretable machine learning models, fairness-aware algorithms, and tools for AI auditing and accountability.
XAI · Fairness · Trust
🤖
Reinforcement Learning
Multi-agent systems, robotics control, game-playing agents, optimization problems, and real-world RL deployment challenges.
RL · Robotics · Optimization
